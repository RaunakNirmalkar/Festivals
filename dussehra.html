DUSSEHRA

Dussehra, also known as Vijayadashami, is one of the major festivals of India. It celebrates the victory of Lord Rama over the demon king Ravana, symbolizing the triumph of good over evil. People celebrate this festival by burning large effigies of Ravana, Meghnath, and Kumbhkaran. The festival also marks the end of Navratri and Durga Puja celebrations in many parts of India.
